# IT114_GROUP3HCI_Project
Food at Your Doorstep is a modern food delivery system designed to provide a convenient and efficient way for customers to order meals from various restaurants and have them delivered to their preferred location. Through a user-friendly website or mobile application, customers can browse menus, customize orders, view ratings, and complete transactions using multiple payment options. The system integrates three core components: a customer interface for ordering, a restaurant interface for managing and preparing orders, and a delivery management system that allows the assigning of couriers. Additional features such as notifications, live analytics and estimated delivery times enhance the overall user experience. By connecting customers, restaurants, and delivery personnel on a single platform, the system expands business reach while offering users greater accessibility and convenience in today’s fast-paced digital environment.
